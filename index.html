    Why India?

    Largest Talent Pool

    50 million students pass out from college in India every year. That is 5x the population of Israel, 90% for the UK, and 15% of the population of the US added to the job market every year. You can’t beat the scale anywhere in the world.

    Time Zone Advantage

    India has the time zone advantage for any country willing to tap the Asian market. India is 2.5 hours behind Singapore and HK and 3.5 hrs behind Japan. So India can be your perfect delivery centre to service all of these markets.

    Cultural Fit

    Indian workforce is characterized as hard-working and service-oriented. The Indian workforce is a great cultural fit with any region – US, UK or Israel combined with excellent English skills.

    English

    The Indian education system places a significant emphasis on English skills. So Indian students have been fluent in English since an early stage. India is a services economy and is home to several of the world’s largest technology companies – TCS ($200B market cap), Infosys ($100Bn market cap), etc. 7 Indian companies feature in the 2021 Fortune 500 companies of India.

    Top Class Talent

    The supply of 50 million graduates and undergraduates keeps the compensation levels low as there is tough competition for every job. Additionally, top companies use India as a development and R&D centre. This ensures that the talent continues to upskill for all requirements.
